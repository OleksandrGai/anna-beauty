About the Artist

Hello, I'm Anna

I am a professional hair and makeup artist based in Kyiv, Ukraine, with a deep passion for enhancing natural beauty. With years of experience in bridal, editorial, and evening looks, I bring precision, creativity, and care to every client I work with.

My philosophy is simple: beauty should feel effortless. Whether you're walking down the aisle, stepping onto a red carpet, or celebrating a special moment, I create looks that complement your personality and make you feel like the best version of yourself.

I use only premium products and stay at the forefront of beauty trends to ensure every look is both timeless and modern.

— Anna Gailiunas
